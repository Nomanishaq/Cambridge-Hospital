About Your Hospital Bill



Please be aware that each time you receive services from the hospital, a separate account is created. It is possible to have several accounts open at the same time.



Insurance companies listed on the front of your statement will be billed as a service to you. Insurance benefits will depend on your policy and remaining deductible. Any amounts not covered by insurance are the responsibility of the guarantor or responsible party.




You may receive additional bills from physicians who helped you with your care while you were a patient at Danbury Hospital. Should you have any questions concerning their bill, please contact them directly.
